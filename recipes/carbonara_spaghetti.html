Carbonara Spaghetti

Description

An easy-to-make carbonara spaghetti. Sharing with your friends is a must! This recipe is suitable for students living in hostels.

Recipe Details

Portion : 3-4 People

Preparing Time : 5 minutes

Cooking Time : 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 1/4 butter
  • 1/2 diced yellow onion
  • 4 chopped garlic
  • 2 chopped chillies
  • sliced sausages
  • 1 can of carbonara sauce
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the butter in pan.
  3. Put in onion, garlic, and chillies. Saute until wilted.
  4. Put in sausages and stir.
  5. After the sausages turned a bit brown, put in carbonara sauce and stir.
  6. Put in milk and stir. Then let it sit to make the sauce thicker.
  7. Then put the cheese in.
  8. Optional: you can mix the boiled spaghetti together with the sauce.
  9. Serve to eat.
