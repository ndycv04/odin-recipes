Tuna Casserole

Description

This tuna casserole taste like home. The smell when baking it makes you crave it before you even eat it.

Recipe Details

Portion : 2-4 People

Preparing Time : 10 minutes

Cooking Time : 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g of macaroni
  • 2 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 1/2 diced yellow onion
  • 2 chopped garlic
  • black pepper powder
  • 2 tbsp of flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1 can of mushroom soup
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1/2 and 1 cups of mozarella cheese
  • breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in pan.
  3. Put in onion and garlic. Saute until wilted.
  4. Add a pinch of black pepper powder. Then, add flour and stir.
  5. Put in milk, tuna, and mushroom soup. Stir until bubbly.
  6. Put in the macaroni and stir.
  7. Put in 1 cup of cheese and stir until cheese is fully melt.
  8. When done, switch off the stove and set aside.
  9. Set an aluminium tray. Put in the macaroni inside the tray.
  10. On top of the macaroni, sprinkle the remaining cheese and some breadcrumbs.
  11. Put the tray in an air fryer. Bake with 170 degree temperature for 7 mins.
  12. Serve to eat.
