Cheesy Potatoes

Description

This is a quick and simple potato recipe that will melt everyone. You can make this when you randomly get a late-night cravings. This recipe is suitable for students living in hostels.

Recipe Details

Portion : 1-3 People

Preparing Time : 5 minutes

Cooking Time : 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g of sliced potatoes
  • cooking oil
  • 1 cup of mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in pan.
  2. Fry the potatoes in the pan.
  3. When done, set aside.
  4. Set an aluminium tray. Arrange the potatoes neatly inside.
  5. Arrange the potatoes neatly inside.
  6. Then, sprinkle some cheese on top.
  7. Repeat Step 5 and 6 until the tray is full.
  8. Put the tray in an air fryer. Bake with 180 degree temperature for 7 mins.
  9. Servet to eat.
