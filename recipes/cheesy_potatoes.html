Cheesy Potatoes

Description

This is a quick and simple cheesy potato recipe. Perfect for your late-night cravings.

Recipe Details

Portion : 1-3 People

Preparing Time : 5 minutes

Cooking Time : 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g of sliced potatoes
  • cooking oil
  • 1 cup of mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in pan.
  2. Fry the potatoes in the pan.
  3. When done, set aside.
  4. Set an aluminium tray.
  5. Arrange the potatoes neatly inside by layer.
  6. Then, sprinkle some cheese on top, also by layer.
  7. Repeat Step 5 and 6 until the tray is full.
  8. Put the tray in an air fryer. Bake with 180-degree temperature for 7 mins.
  9. Serve to eat.
